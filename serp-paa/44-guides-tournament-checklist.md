# SERP/Question Gap Framework - Tournament Checklist

Captured: 2026-06-02

## Keyword

tournament checklist

## Semrush Evidence

- Exact US volume: 20
- `how to organize a tournament`: 20
- `how to run a tournament`: 20

## Intent

Users want an organizer workflow for preparing, scheduling, communicating, and reviewing a tournament.

## Required Coverage

- Participant list.
- Format selection.
- Seeding, byes, tie-breakers.
- Venue/time constraints.
- Schedule generation and review.
- Communication and print/export.
- Post-event notes.

## Risk Notes

- Do not claim the site handles registration, payment, live scores, or standings accounts.
- Keep the checklist operational and concrete.
