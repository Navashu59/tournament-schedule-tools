## User Problem

Organizers need a fair tie-breaker order for round robin standings before teams finish with the same record.

## Required Angle

Teach common tie-breaker methods and how to publish them before the event. Do not claim one universal official order.

4. Points scored and points allowed
5. Strength of schedule or record against tied teams
