# Full Launch Brief

## Positioning

Tournament Schedule Tools helps organizers make fair tournament brackets, round robin schedules, and league fixtures without spreadsheets.

## Differentiation

The site must not be a generic bracket maker. It wins by supporting fairness and scheduling constraints:

- odd participants and byes
- round robin generation
- court / venue / time slot assignment
- printable output
- CSV export
- copyable schedule text
- no signup
- local-first workflow

## MVP

Launch 30 pages plus homepage, tools hub, trust pages, sitemap, robots, and 404.

P0 tools:

1. Tournament Schedule Maker
2. Round Robin Generator
3. Tournament Bracket Maker
4. League Schedule Maker

P0 outputs:

- bracket preview
- round list
- match list
- fairness summary
- print view
- CSV export
- copy schedule
