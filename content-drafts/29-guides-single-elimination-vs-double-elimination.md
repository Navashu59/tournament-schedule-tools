# Single Elimination vs Double Elimination

## Tool Intro

Pick the right tournament format fast. Use our free scheduler to generate a single or double elimination bracket in seconds. No signup needed. Just enter your teams or players, choose your format, and get a printable bracket with byes handled automatically.

## How To Use It

1. Enter your teams or players (any number, odd or even).
2. Select **Single Elimination** or **Double Elimination**.
3. Click **Generate**.
4. View your bracket, assign courts/venues and time slots.
5. Print, copy the schedule link, or export as CSV.

## Example

You have 7 teams for a weekend basketball tournament.  
- **Single elimination**: 3 rounds, 6 games. One loss and you're out.  
- **Double elimination**: 4 rounds, 12-13 games. Teams get a second chance in the losers' bracket.  
Both formats handle the odd number by adding byes automatically.

## What The Results Mean

- **Single elimination**: Fastest path to a winner. Every match eliminates one team.  
- **Double elimination**: Fairer but longer. A team must lose twice to be eliminated. The winners' bracket champion gets a slight advantage (needs to win one fewer match in the final).  
- **Byes**: Shown as empty slots in round 1. They're assigned to top seeds or randomly.

## Common Mistakes

- **Ignoring byes**: With an odd number of teams, byes are necessary. Don't try to force a full bracket.  
- **Wrong format for your time**: Double elimination takes nearly twice as many matches. Check your venue availability first.  
- **Forgetting the losers' bracket**: In double elimination, the losers' bracket keeps the tournament alive. Make sure you have enough time slots.

## Common Questions

**What is the simplest rule?**  
Single elimination: lose once, you're out. Double elimination: lose twice, you're out.

**When should I use this format?**  
Use single elimination for quick tournaments (e.g., a one-day event). Use double elimination when fairness matters more than speed (e.g., a league qualifier).

**What mistakes should I avoid?**  
Don't use double elimination if you have limited time or venues. Don't forget to assign byes for odd numbers.

**Can I use a generator instead of a spreadsheet?**  
Yes. Our tool generates the bracket instantly. No manual seeding or complex formulas.

**What should I print or export?**  
Print the bracket view for your venue wall. Export CSV for digital records or sharing with participants. Copy the schedule link for easy access.
